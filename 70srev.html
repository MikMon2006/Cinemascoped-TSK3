Love the 70s though



That '70s Show is a nostalgic sitcom set in the 1970s that centres on Eric Forman, a teenager living in suburban Wisconsin, and his tight-knit group of friends: Donna, Kelso, Jackie, Hyde, and Fez. They spend most of their time hanging out in Eric’s basement, navigating the ups and downs of adolescence, relationships, and growing up all while surrounded by the pop culture, fashion, and social changes of the decade.

The show blends humour with coming-of-age themes, highlighting teenage rebellion, family dynamics, and friendship. Eric's strict but lovable parents, Red and Kitty, provide a grounded counterpoint to the group’s antics. With its mix of quirky characters, iconic one-liners, and a throwback vibe, That '70s Show became a cult favourite, celebrating the awkward, funny, and formative moments of teenage life.
