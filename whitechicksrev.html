Man... I Feel Like a Woman

Columbia Pictures | Keenen Ivory Wayans | 2004 | 15+


White Chicks is a comedy about two Black FBI agents, Kevin and Marcus Copeland, who go undercover in an outrageous way. After failing to protect two wealthy white heiresses, Brittany and Tiffany Wilson, from a kidnapping attempt, the agents are given a final chance to prove themselves.

When the real Wilson sisters back out of public appearances due to minor injuries, Kevin and Marcus take their place, disguising themselves with full makeup and prosthetics to pose as the socialites. Their mission is to uncover who is behind the kidnapping plot while blending into the world of Hamptons high society. As they navigate elite parties, jealous rivals, and romantic complications, the agents struggle to keep their cover intact. Along the way, they gain unexpected insights into the lives of the women they’re impersonating. The film mixes slapstick comedy, fish-out-of-water scenarios, and social satire. Despite negative reviews from critics, it became a cult favourite thanks to its outrageous humour and memorable scenes.

This is such an insane movie; I just love it. The way I see it and can quote half of it shows how timeless it is.
