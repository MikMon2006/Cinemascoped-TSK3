Idk Man I just Love it



How I Met Your Mother is a sitcom that follows Ted Mosby as he tells his children the story of how he met their mother. Set in New York City, the show centres on Ted and his close friends; Marshall, Lily, Barney, and Robin, as they navigate careers, relationships, and adulthood. Over nine seasons, Ted’s romantic journey is filled with ups and downs until he finally meets Tracy, the woman he marries and has children with. Tragically, she dies in 2024. The series ends with Ted, years later, possibly rekindling a romance with Robin. Blending humour, heart, and a unique narrative style, the show explores love, timing, and the unpredictability of life.

I don’t really know why I like this show and have watched it approximately 10 times, but something about the characters that just feels like free therapy. All the characters are kind of insane in their own way and it is just funny and comforting to me. 5 out of 5 popcorns…
