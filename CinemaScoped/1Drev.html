Boyband Obsessed

SYCO Entertainment | Morgan Spurlock | 2013 | PG


One Direction: This Is Us (2013) is a 3D documentary and concert film directed by Morgan Spurlock that offers an intimate look at the meteoric rise of the British Irish boy band One Direction. The film chronicles the journey of Niall Horan, Zayn Malik, Liam Payne, Harry Styles, and Louis Tomlinson from their individual auditions on The X Factor to forming a band under Simon Cowell's mentorship and achieving global stardom.

Through a blend of live concert footage from their 2012–2013 world tour and behind-the-scenes moments, the documentary displays Sthe group's dynamic performances and provides insights into their personal lives. Viewers are taken on a journey that includes visits to the members' hometowns, interactions with their families, and candid discussions about the challenges and pressures of fame. The film also features appearances by notable figures such as Simon Cowell, Cristiano Ronaldo, Chris Rock, and Martin Scorsese.

This Is Us not only highlights the band's musical talents and onstage charisma but also delves into their camaraderie and the strong bond they share, offering fans and newcomers alike a comprehensive look at what it means to be part of One Direction.
