I do Love a Good Ghost Show



School Spirits is a teen supernatural drama that follows Maddie Nears, a high school student who finds herself stuck in the afterlife after mysteriously disappearing and being presumed dead. Trapped in her school as a ghost, Maddie joins a group of other teen spirits who also died on campus, all unable to move on.

While grappling with her new reality, Maddie tries to uncover the truth about her own death. As she investigates, she learns secrets about her friends, her boyfriend, and the school itself, realizing that the circumstances of her disappearance are more complex and sinister than she thought.

Blending mystery, emotional drama, and supernatural elements, School Spirits explores themes of grief, identity, and unfinished business, all through the lens of a teenager caught between life and death.

Okay so, Maddie is kinda too nonchalant for me but that’s okay all the characters are very insufferable. BUT. Milo Manheim, the man of the hour. The guy who plays Wally the 80S JOCK. It is pretty self- explanatory at this point. Anywayssss… I like a show centred around ghosts and spirits I can’t lie.
